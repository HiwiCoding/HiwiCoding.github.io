About Me

I am a UI/UX designer with a strong foundation in architecture and fashion design. My background in physical space and fabric aesthetics deeply informs how I approach digital product design. I believe design is about storytelling and problem-solving through empathy and clarity. Over the past few years, I've worked on digital products that require detailed research, user journey mapping, wireframes, interactive prototypes, and final interfaces that delight users.

I thrive in cross-functional teams and enjoy turning complex problems into intuitive and elegant user interfaces. My curiosity keeps me learning continuously — exploring behavioral design, accessibility, and the intersection between creativity and code.
